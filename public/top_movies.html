Best Movies

Arnold's top 3 movies

Batman The Dark Knight

Amazing openning sequence, great character establishment.
Fights are well choreographed, suspense is immense whenever joker is on screen.
Fun Fact: Christian bale was asked to be batman for the DCEU however
because Christopher Nolan was unavailable to direct, he refused.

EX Machina (animated)

Watched this movie when I was young and thoroughly enjoyed it.
From what I can remember it is regarding the progression of technology in a
new age where people are already cybernetically enhanced.
However, new technology has lead to a hijacking of these cybernetics in people

Superbad

This movie is a cultural icon. Superbad follows 3 highschool kids on the verge of graduation
Realizing this new chapter of life will leave them seperated unable to spend
everyday together hanging out and having fun, they decide to try party and rizz girls
Great journey and probably one of the last genuinely funny movies before everything went woke
